About GSNOOK

GSNOOK is a scientific consulting and data analytics initiative focused on computational biology, epidemiology, environmental health and reproducible scientific workflows.

The project integrates biological sciences, biostatistics, bioinformatics and One Health perspectives to support research, technical consultancy and evidence-based decision making.

Areas of interest include environmental epidemiology, zoonotic diseases, aquaculture health, scientific data analysis and biomedical interpretation.
